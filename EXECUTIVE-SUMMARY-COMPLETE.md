## 📊 Expected Pedagogical Impact

### StratégieMalo — Mental Calculation Strategy

**Before**: 
- 8+7 = counting on fingers (15 seconds)
- Mental math errors: 40-50%

**After (2 weeks)**:
- 8+7 = 8+2+5 = 10+5 = 15 (3 seconds, mental)
- Mental math errors: <10%

**Transfer**: Problem-solving speed up 40%+ in real homework

---

### ComprendreTexte — Reading Comprehension

**Before**:
- 30-40% kids "read but don't understand"
- Text comprehension questions: 50% success rate

**After (2 weeks)**:
- Kids can answer "Pourquoi?" without rereading
- Text comprehension questions: 90%+ success rate

**Transfer**: Literary analysis in class becomes accessible

---

### OrthoPuzzle — Spelling Patterns

**Before**:
- Homophones: 55% confusion (et/est mixed up frequently)
- Plurals: forget S, get confused with -aux exceptions
- Accords: "tout le monde sont venus" ❌

**After (2 weeks)**:
- Homophones: 90%+ accuracy
- Plurals: apply rules consistently
- Accords: "tout le monde est venu" ✅

**Transfer**: Dictation errors drop by 40%+ on homophones/pluriels
